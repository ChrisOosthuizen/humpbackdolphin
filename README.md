Abundance of Indian Ocean humpback dolphins (Sousa plumbea) along the Garden Route of South Africa.

Here, we estimate the abundance and group structure of Indian Ocean humpback dolphins along the Garden Route section of the south coast of South Africa. We conducted boat-based photo-identification surveys over 16 months (March 2014–June 2015) and applied a Bayesian open-population (Jolly-Seber) model to encounter histories of 65 identified individuals.
